Welcome To Chai jee and Waffle jee

chai jee is one of the most loved beverges in the world , especially in india. It is made by brewing black tea with aromatic species like cardamom and ginger. A cup of hot chaii is not just a drink but an emotion that brings comfort and freshness.

Waffles are popular breakfast and dessert item made for a better cooked between two patterned plates that give them a unique texture. They are crispy on the outside and soft of the inside . Waffles are often enjoyed with topping like lotus biscoff, butterscotch , choclate and blueberry waffles.
